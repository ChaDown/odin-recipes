Delicious Lasagna Recipe

This classic italian dish is a heartwarming homemade recipe that can be made in less than an hour. It's a sure fire special in any home and it's bound to leave everybody at the table happily stuffed full of goodness

With just some simple ingredients, some careful preparation and a little sprinkle of love, you'll have everyone asking you for your special recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 Lasagna Sheets
  • 500g ground beef mince
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 large brown onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup of milk

    • Steps

    1. Fry onion until golden
    2. Add the mince and finely diced carrot and cook until browned
    3. Add in the diced garlic for the last minute
    4. Pour in the can of tomatoes and simmer for ten minutes
    5. While it simmers, whisk together melted butter, milk and flour to make our bechamel sauce
    6. Spoon a thin layer of the tomato mix into a tray. Add sheets of lasagna above. Repeat for as many layers as you desire.
    7. Finish by adding a creamy layer of bechamel to the top and covering it with grated mozzarella
    8. Bake for 30 mins at 180 until cheeze is lightly browned
